📘 Smart Waste Learning Hub

🌱 Organic Waste

Organic waste decomposes naturally and returns nutrients to the soil.

  • Food waste
  • Vegetables
  • Fruits
  • Leaves
Benefits:
  • Composting 🌿
  • Improves soil fertility
  • Environment-friendly

♻️ Recyclable Waste

These materials can be processed and reused to create new products.

  • Plastic bottles
  • Paper
  • Glass
  • Metal
Benefits:
  • Saves natural resources
  • Reduces pollution
  • Energy efficient

🚯 Non-Recyclable Waste

Waste that cannot be reused or recycled easily.

  • Sanitary waste
  • Food wrappers
  • Styrofoam
Problems:
  • Landfills increase
  • Environmental pollution
  • Long decomposition time

⚠️ Hazardous Waste

Dangerous waste that can harm humans and the environment.

  • Batteries
  • Chemicals
  • Medical waste
Risks:
  • Health problems
  • Water and soil contamination
  • Toxic exposure
