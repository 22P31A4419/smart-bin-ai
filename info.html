ℹ What is Waste Management?

Waste management involves collecting, processing, and disposing waste efficiently. It helps reduce pollution and protect the environment.

⚠ Effects of Poor Waste Management

  • 🌫 Air Pollution

    Burning waste releases harmful gases causing diseases and global warming.

  • 💧 Water Pollution

    Waste contaminates water harming aquatic life and humans.

  • 🌱 Soil Pollution

    Chemicals reduce soil fertility and crop productivity.

  • 🦠 Health Issues

    Waste spreads diseases like dengue and malaria.

💡 Solutions

  • ♻ Segregation

    Separate wet and dry waste at home.

  • 🔄 Recycling

    Recycling reduces pollution and saves resources.

  • 🚫 Reduce Plastic

    Avoid single-use plastics.

  • 🌱 Composting

    Convert kitchen waste into fertilizer.
