🌱 Composting

📌 What is Composting?

Composting is a natural biological process in which microorganisms break down organic waste into nutrient-rich fertilizer. It reduces landfill waste and improves soil fertility.

🔬 How Composting Works?

  • 🦠 Microorganisms break waste
  • 🌡 Heat is generated
  • 💨 Oxygen supports process
  • 💧 Moisture is required

♻️ Types of Waste

  • 🍌 Fruit peels
  • 🍚 Food waste
  • 🍂 Leaves
  • ☕ Coffee
  • 🥚 Eggshells

⚙️ Composting Steps

  1. Collect waste
  2. Add dry leaves
  3. Maintain moisture
  4. Mix regularly
  5. Wait 2–6 weeks

🌟 Benefits

  • 🌍 Reduces pollution
  • 🌱 Improves soil
  • 💧 Retains water
  • ♻️ Eco-friendly
